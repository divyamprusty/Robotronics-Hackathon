Educable

A path to education

Do you want to help people, do social service or stand up for a cause? Does your heart melt when you do something good for others?

Educable is a platform aimed for those with disablities to get access to a higher level of education than they can regularly afford. You can sign up as a tutor to do a good deed, and help educate children with visual, physical or learning disabilities. If you are a disabled student, or are a guardian of a child with disablities and looking for a better way to educate them, you can sign up to be a student and choose a teacher.

Everyone has the right to education, so why deprive those who cannot afford or have a problem in studying in normal schools? We want everyone to be treated equally and have a fair chance at being educated and living this world in a better way.

What are the perks of a Donator?

Once you donate a certain amount of money to our organisation you would automatically become a member of our organisation and would get the following advantages :-

  • You would be invited to charity events conducted by our organisation.
  • You will get a free tour of the orphanages and will get to meet the children whose lives would have changes because of you little contributions.
  • You will be invited to all our webinars and help us with our decision making.
  • You will be provided with a gift at the end of every year you remain with us throughout and support us.
